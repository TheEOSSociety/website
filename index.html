The story of how we got started on
The EOS Society

The EOS Society was started by a student of University of Lucknow, Sarvajeet Shukla on September 23rd, 2023

September 20th, 2023
Founder Sarvajeet Shukla in this first year of Bachelors in Arts, along with his batchmates started this debating society.
September 23rd, 2023
The EOS Society conducts its first debate on Women Empowerment Bill 2023, in Room no. 234 - Political Science Department.
October 3rd, 2023
Functioning continues as it conducts the second debate on India's influence in G20 as a voice of global south.

This session was important for the foundation of the society because the current permanent members of the society joined the club starting this point.

November 4th, 2023
A fruitful debate with lots of qualified speakers on Climate change and Development and a series of debates go on and on until the next year.
August 9th, 2024
Ahead of India's 78th Independence day, the society conducted a large scale Quiz Competition on Kakori Incident.

The event boosted a lot of reach and functioning of the society and introduced us to new scholars along with the guidance of renowned professors and experts on their fields.

September 9th-10th, 2024
The EOS Society proudly concluded the convocation week of the college with its first ever Mock Parliament session and garnered a huge success in collaboration with cultural department of the University.

The college was familiarized with the motto "Let the Thoughts come out".

September 23rd, 2024
The EOS Society completes its first year of establishment and conducts its empowering debate with the freshers in the presence of esteemed Heads of Departments and Professors.
October 15th, 2024
Samvad Series starts.

A simple yet helpful new - learn and grow through discussions - initiative takes place.

October 21st, 2024
Committees and Team of the society expands and now seeks milestones that were earlier just far-fetched
